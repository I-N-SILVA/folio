# Folio — Marketing Copy Kit

Ready-to-use, on-brand copy for launch. Pair with `LAUNCH.md` (Product Hunt /
app stores) and `APPSUMO_LAUNCH.md` (lifetime-deal GTM). Voice: see `BRAND.md`.

---

## 1. Positioning

- **Category:** Interactive digital publishing / flipbook software.
- **For:** creators, studios, agencies, and teams who publish catalogs,
  lookbooks, portfolios, reports, and editorial.
- **Against:** flat PDF download links and generic flipbook embeds.
- **Value:** turn a static PDF into an interactive, branded, measurable
  experience your readers actually finish.

## 2. Taglines

- Make every page feel collected.
- Digital publishing with soul.
- Your PDF deserves more than a download link.
- Turn any PDF into an interactive flipbook readers actually finish.
- Publish beautifully. Measure everything.

## 3. Elevator pitches

- **One sentence:** Folio turns static PDFs into immersive, interactive
  flipbooks with hotspots, analytics, and one-line embeds.
- **One paragraph:** Folio is a studio and reader for premium digital
  publications. Import a PDF or build page-by-page, layer in hotspots and lead
  gates, publish a hosted reader or embed it anywhere, and watch real engagement
  — opens, dwell time, completion, hotspot clicks — flow into your own data
  layer. Built for portfolios, catalogs, lookbooks, reports, and editorial.

## 4. Value props (with proof)

1. **Tactile reader** — a polished 3D page-turn that feels editorial, not like a
   PDF viewer.
2. **Interactive hotspots** — tag products, video, and links right onto the page.
3. **Reader intelligence** — opens, dwell time, completion, hotspot clicks.
4. **PDF → experience** — import an existing PDF and ship the same afternoon.
5. **Elegant embeds** — one line into any landing page, store, or CMS.
6. **Brand control** — themes, custom domains, and no watermark on paid plans.
